The house rules

Honest food.
No fine print.

We keep the promise simple enough to see from the counter: fresh prep, real cooking and nothing carried into tomorrow.

01🥬

Morning starts here

Our team washes, chops and preps produce from scratch each day. No tubs of yesterday’s cut vegetables.

02🤲

Made by hands

From patties to dips and dressings, the little variations tell you a person—not a production line—made your food.

03♻

Nothing goes back

What remains unsold is donated to Devdoot Food Bank. Today’s food should do good today.

Real ingredients behave naturally

Season-led.
Made right here.

Beetroot changes shade. Handmade patties vary in shape. Seasonal fruit tastes a little different from week to week. We see those differences as signs of real food—not flaws to hide.

01
Fresh-prep rhythmProduce is washed and cut each day, with no pre-cut vegetables stored overnight.
02
Nothing factory-perfectReal hands shape patties and real seasons influence colour, texture and flavour.
03
Prepared in-houseGuacamole, salsa, sour cream, tzatziki and other components are made fresh by the kitchen.
